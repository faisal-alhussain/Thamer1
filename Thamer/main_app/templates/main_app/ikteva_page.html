What is ikteva?

The NTCs initiative was launched back in 2008 as part of a strategic partnership between Saudi Aramco and Technical & Vocational Training Corp. (TVTC) to build National Training Centers (NTCs) that would qualify young Saudis and provide employment opportunities for them in various industry sectors through a sponsorship scheme that provides training followed by employment. The NTCs provide a key platform that strengthens the impact of In-Kingdom Total Value Add (IKTVA) through providing market tailored training to support workforce development, and directly contribute to Saudization.
